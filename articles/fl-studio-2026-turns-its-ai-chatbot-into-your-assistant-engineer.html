FL Studio 2026 turns its AI chatbot into your assistant engineer

  • Image Line於2026年推出FL Studio，將AI聊天機器人Gopher升級為助理工程師，能夠執行用戶指令。
  • Gopher可以執行音樂製作指令，但無法創建自動化、插入音符或選擇插件中的特定預設。
  • 新版FL Studio引入重建的Flex虛擬樂器，提供多種音效包，並改善了預設瀏覽器的過濾器和類別。
  • FL Cloud訂閱者可享自動雲備份功能，並新增“音頻記錄器”，可捕捉主輸出60秒音頻。

原文節錄

Last year, Image Line introduced Gopher for FL Studio, an AI chatbot that was basically a glorified instruction manual. You asked it how to do something, and it would serve up the relevant instructions. It’s the kind of thing I actually use AI for on a semi-regular basis. But in the new release, Gopher can actually execute actions on your behalf.
