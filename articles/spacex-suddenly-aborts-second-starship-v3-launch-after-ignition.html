SpaceX suddenly aborts second Starship V3 launch after ignition

  • SpaceX於南德克薩斯州的發射場進行第二次Starship V3發射嘗試，但在引擎點火後突然中止發射。
  • CEO馬斯克表示，因為部分引擎未啟動，觸發自動中止發射，並希望在幾天內再次嘗試。
  • 此次發射是SpaceX自6月12日公開上市以來的首次Starship測試發射，該公司在IPO中籌集超過850億美元。
  • 發射中止後，SpaceX的股價在盤後交易中下跌超過4%，收盤價低於IPO價格135美元。

原文節錄

SpaceX abruptly aborted the second attempted launch of its upgraded Starship rocket system on Thursday, just moments after the booster ignited at the company’s complex in South Texas. CEO Elon Musk said in a post on X that “[s]ome of the engines didn’t start, triggering an automatic launch abort” and that the company would try again “hopefully in a few days.
