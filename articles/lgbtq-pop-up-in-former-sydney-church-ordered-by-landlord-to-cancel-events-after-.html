LGBTQ+ pop-up in former Sydney church ordered by landlord to cancel events after religious protest

  • 位於悉尼的前教堂Divine Playhouse因宗教團體抗議而被房東要求取消活動，並停止“從事冒犯性貿易”。
  • 該場地於上星期三開幕，旨在為藝術家提供安全和包容的空間，並在開幕前更名以回應基督教社群的擔憂。

原文節錄

A deconsecrated Sydney church newly transformed into an LGBTQ+ venue has had to cancel events and been ordered to stop “engaging in offensive trade” by its landlord after religious groups protested against its opening night. Divine Playhouse opened last Wednesday with the intention of being a safe and inclusive space for artists to work and connect with audiences, its organiser said.
