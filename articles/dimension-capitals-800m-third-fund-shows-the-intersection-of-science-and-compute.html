Dimension Capital’s $800M third fund shows the intersection of science and compute is booming

  • Dimension Capital於2022年底成立，近日宣佈籌集到8億美元的第三支基金，較18個月前的5億美元第二支基金增長60%
  • 創始人Zavian Dar、Adam Goulburn及Nan Li預測創業者將越來越多地建立跨越生物科技和軟件的深科技公司
  • 2024年，Dimension Capital共同領投Chai Discovery的3000萬美元種子投資，該初創公司專注於開發藥物的開源AI基礎模型
  • 其他投資包括反衰老初創公司New Limit及推理公司Modal Labs，並在Anthropic收購其投資組合公司Coefficient Bio後獲得股份

原文節錄

While many newer VC firms are still struggling to raise fresh capital, Dimension Capital, founded in late 2022, has been raising increasingly larger funds in quick succession. On Tuesday, the New York firm — which invests at the intersection of science and compute — announced an $800 million fund, which is 60% larger than its $500 million second vehicle announced just 18 months ago.
