Telstra glitches continue as ‘secondary’ outage hits triple-zero calls and regional trains remain stranded

  • 特爾斯特拉公司確認，周三的全國性故障導致的次要問題，仍影響顧客撥打緊急電話「000」的能力，部分顧客收到錯誤信息，電話嘗試連接其他網絡。
  • 澳洲聯邦通訊部長安妮卡·威爾斯表示，雖然問題已大致解決，但仍有殘留問題，並提到部分電話直接轉至語音信箱。

  • 特爾斯特拉的首席執行官表示，故障期間撥打「000」的電話數量異常增加，並呼籲顧客在故障期間不要重複撥打緊急電話。

原文節錄

Telstra customers are still having trouble calling triple zero on Thursday morning as the telecommunications giant confirmed a secondary issue stemming from Wednesday’s national outage continued to impact calls. Some who tried to call the emergency hotline were receiving an error message and their phones would try to connect to an alternative network, Telstra said.
