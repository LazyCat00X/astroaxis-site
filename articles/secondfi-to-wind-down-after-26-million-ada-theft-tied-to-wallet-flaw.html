SecondFi to Wind Down After $2.6 Million ADA Theft Tied to Wallet Flaw

  • Cardano 錢包 SecondFi 宣佈將停止服務，因為其軟件漏洞導致約 2.6 百萬美元的 16.1 百萬 ADA 被盜。
  • 攻擊者利用漏洞從交易數據中推導用戶的私鑰，但 SecondFi 強調 Cardano 網絡本身並未受到影響。
  • 獨立調查顯示主要攻擊者為一個資金雄厚的團體，部分跡象指向北韓的 Lazarus Group，但尚未確認。
  • 用戶對資金恢復的期待感到失望，SecondFi 現在計劃在八月推出零知識恢復工具和錢包導出功能。

原文節錄

Cardano wallet SecondFi said it will wind down its own service and the Yoroi wallet it took over, roughly a month after attackers exploited a flaw in its software to steal about 16.1 million ADA, worth around $2.6 million, from 374 wallets. The company confirmed the shutdown in an update this week, saying it will not resume normal operations even though the vulnerability has been patched.
