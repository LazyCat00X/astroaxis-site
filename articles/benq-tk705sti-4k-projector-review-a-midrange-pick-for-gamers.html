BenQ TK705STi 4K Projector Review: A Midrange Pick for Gamers

  • BenQ TK705STi是一款短距離投影機，專為遊戲玩家設計，具備低延遲性能和較大屏幕尺寸。
  • 該投影機的投射比為0.8:1，能在距離牆面約8英尺的距離投射出10英尺寬的影像。
  • 雖然對於遊戲表現尚可，但在電影和電視節目方面的畫質表現一般，與高端產品相比存在差距。
  • 使用過程中，投影機的穩定性不足，且自動校正功能存在一些故障。

原文節錄

Home cinema projectors are all over the map in terms of price and performance. It’s often the case that you get what you pay for. High-end projectors that mimic what you see at the local cineplex can cost upwards of $6,000. On the lower end, you could easily pay less than $1,000 for a home projector, but you may sacrifice brightness, contrast, and color quality.
